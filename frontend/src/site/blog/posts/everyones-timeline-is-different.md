One thing I've truly realized in the past few months is this:

**What you genuinely dream of will come to you one day — if you keep working for it, consistently.**

During my engineering days, I always dreamt of achieving certain things in my career. But when I
graduated last year (2025), I felt disappointed, because I hadn't reached any of those milestones
yet.

At one point it even felt like the people around me were getting the things I had dreamed of —
and getting them much earlier than me.

But almost a year later, I finally started achieving what I once only imagined.

And this journey taught me something I keep coming back to:

> **Everyone's timeline is different.**

Sometimes your friends or family reach their goals earlier. Sometimes life just feels unfair. But
that doesn't mean your time will never come.

If you keep learning, keep improving, and keep showing up every single day, you will eventually
reach that milestone too.

So keep working.  
Keep believing.  
And **never stop** chasing what you genuinely **dream** of.
